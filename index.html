    I am a Mechanical Engineer graduated from UTN FRBB, experienced in CAD systems such as AutoCAD, CADWorx Plant, SolidWorks, Autodesk Inventor. My professional and academic interests extend over a large range of fields, being CAD/CAE Design applied to energy systems one of particular interest.     Projects I have worked on

    In the past two years, I have worked in the development of piping systems for transportation of a wide range of fluids, at various conditions, for petrochemical and Oil&Gas industries.
