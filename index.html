    Hi, I am Maximiliano Herrero.

    I am a Mechanical Engineer graduated from UTN FRBB, experienced in CAD systems such as AutoCAD, CADWorx Plant, SolidWorks, Autodesk Inventor. My professional and academic interests extend over a large range of fields, being CAD/CAE Design applied to energy systems one of particular interest. Engineering Design requires constant learning and testing, which can be very challenging and helps feed one's CURIOSITY.

    Projects I have worked on

    In the past two years, I have worked in the development of piping systems for transportation of a wide range of fluids, at various conditions, for petrochemical and Oil&Gas industries. Before that period, I was involved in the conception and manufacturing of underground mine access and emergency exit systems, and safety work lift platforms and cages. Another industry field in which I was able to develop my experience is the food industry, where storage and transportation of refined salt ready for packaging was the main subject. I am always open to new experiences, prone to discover and learn new things constantly.
