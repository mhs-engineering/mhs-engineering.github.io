About the job

The project was a joint work between the engineering and construction departments of BAIPRO Ingeniería & Construcción S.A.
It consisted in the design of a firefighting system to protect a new 1500 kVA transformer ready for installation. The system included a perimeter spray system, a control post for the deluge system and the fire water piping (aerial and underground).
